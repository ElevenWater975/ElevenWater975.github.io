BlockPro's official rules

We want to keep BlockPro safe and fair for everyone. Which is why we have rules!


Before you play on blockpro, we have a set of rules that you need to follow

1. Be kind

2. No racism/sexism/homophobia or anything that is offensive.

3. No cheating or hacking is allowed on the server. If you cheat you will be perma banned

4. Doing anything illegal here will not be tolerated. If you are caught doing illegal things (or putting illegal links in chat or even joking about committing crimes) you may be reported to authorities.

5. DDOS is not allowed.

6. Don't share illegal links.

7. Advertising other servers is not allowed here.

8. Gambling on the server is not allowed. This Minecraft server is meant to be a safe server for all ages

Exploiting glitches is not allowed on this server. You may be banned without warning.

Sexual harrassment is not allowed on blockpro.

Failure to follow these rules will result in punishment as in a kick, ban, or mute.

Don't log on to the server with multiple accounts just to break these rules
