Mob Arena info

Mob arena is a co-op minigame the some servers use. This mode has over 10 stages.

Once you reach stage 10, you will begin to fight the boss.

The boss is a cave spider with a lot of health and magical powers like root target, throw nearby, and shoot arrow.

Helpful strategies

Some strategies that I made up are bringing a friend and going to seperate corners where the mobs spawn

Only use healing potions (if you have one) if you are on very low health like 1 /2 hp.

have one friend be an archer beacuse the archer class has a dog that also fights against mobs. Skeletons run away from dogs.
