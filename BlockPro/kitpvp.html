KitPVP information

KitPVP is a Minecraft minigame that lots of servers use for other players to battle each other

If you die in kitpvp, it doesn't mean that you are out of the game, you will respawn and you can get back in the battlefield.

KitPVP isn't just regular pvp, no. KitPVP comes with different types of classes that you can use to battle other players in different ways.

This kitpvp arena was built by Jind

Here's how you start in kitpvp. Fall from spawn down to the battlefield. When you fall, you will not take any fall damage.

Some useful tips

Watch out for players with a more powerful class than yours. If you have a class with full iron, don't try to take on a player with full diamond unless you are a PVP expert.

Strafe by pressing A and D to go left and right so you can try to combo your opponent

W, s, and Shift tapping is very useful. It makes it hard for your opponent to hit you if they are holing W. So you can combo them.
