Requirements to join NJE



Before you join, there are a set of requirements and rules you will need to agree and apply to.

  1. You must be aged 13 or older to join.



    2. If you are going to join the group/gaming clan, you must be aged 13 or over. We need to use this because you are filling an application form which requires information. We do not collect information from persons under 13.

  2. If you are joining the gaming clan then you must have good experience with gaming. Tell us what your skills are in gaming.
