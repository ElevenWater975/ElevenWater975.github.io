Fitness

I believe that physical fitness is important for everyones health. Fitness helps you live longer and helps you be more healthy. I started going to the gym over a year ago when the gyms opened up, but I also work out at home, the gyms are not the only place you should work out. I have been getting more consistent on my workouts. I have been setting a few goals (one of them is to lose a lot of weight) and I want you to as well. Click off this website right now and write down some goals on a piece of paper and when you're done, come back to this website.

This is not me

When you think about what you are going to do for fitness, don't just think about your goals, think of the process on how you're going to achieve those goals. If you just think about your goals, you are probably not going to make much progress. But if you think about the process, you are probably going to make more progress. Never give up on your goals and think about your process. Good luck on your fitness journey.


Meditation

Meditation is important for everyone's mental health. Meditation is supposed to make you more mindful. I believe that everyone should practice meditation. To start meditating, I recommend you download the app "Medito" on your phone. After that, do daily meditation for 3 minutes (I usually do 5 minutes). This is good for your mental health.
